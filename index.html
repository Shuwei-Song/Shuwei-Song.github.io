About

I received my B.Eng. degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2021 and am currently pursuing a PhD in Cyberspace Security at UESTC, advised by Prof. Ting Chen. Since 2023, I have also worked as a Research Assistant in the Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, advised by Prof. Daniel Xiapu Luo.
